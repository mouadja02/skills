# research-and-development

Skills for **deep, rigorous research** — systematic literature reviews, evidence synthesis, and multi-agent research pipelines for any domain.

## Skills in this category

| Skill | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| [`deep-research`](./deep-research/SKILL.md) | Universal deep research agent team. A 13-agent pipeline covering research question formulation, methodology design, systematic literature search, source verification, cross-source synthesis, risk-of-bias assessment, meta-analysis, APA 7.0 report compilation, devil's advocate challenges, ethics review, and post-research monitoring. Seven modes: full research, quick brief, paper review, lit-review, fact-check, Socratic guided research dialogue, and systematic review with optional meta-analysis. |

## When to use this folder

- "Research X" / "do a deep research on Y"
- "Literature review on Z"
- "Systematic review" / "meta-analysis" / "PRISMA"
- "Fact-check this claim"
- "Help me think through this research question"
- "Guide my research" / "Socratic research dialogue"
